
# Project Vision

This project evolves from a simple inverter to a full solar inverter system.

---

## Phase 1 — Low Power Open Loop
- Square wave inverter
- No feedback
- Basic PWM

## Phase 2 — Closed Loop Control
- Voltage sensing
- PWM adjustment
- Stable output

## Phase 3 — Pure Sine Wave
- SPWM control
- LC filter
- Low distortion

## Phase 4 — High Power Design
- Higher wattage
- Thermal design
- Protection circuits

## Phase 5 — Solar Inverter
- MPPT
- Battery management
- Smart control
